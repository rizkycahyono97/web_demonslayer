Kimetsu no Yaiba begins with Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted young man who lives with his family in the mountains. One day, tragedy strikes when his entire family is massacred by demons, leaving his younger sister, Nezuko, as the sole survivor, but has turned into a demon. Tanjiro is determined to cure Nezuko and avenge the destruction of his family.

On his journey, Tanjiro meets Giyu Tomioka, a demon slayer who initially wants to kill Nezuko. However, after seeing Nezuko still protecting her sister even though she has become a demon, Giyu advises Tanjiro to seek help from a mentor, Sakonji Urokodaki. Tanjiro then begins his rigorous training to become a demon slayer.

As a demon slayer, Tanjiro faces various demons with unique abilities. On his mission, he meets fellow fighters such as Zenitsu Agatsuma, who is cowardly but has extraordinary powers when asleep, and Inosuke Hashibira, a wild swordsman who wears a pig's head. Together, they undertake dangerous missions against demons that threaten human life.

Along the way, Tanjiro also discovers that these demons were once human, and each has a sad story. This strengthens his resolve to protect the weak and fight evil without losing his humanity. On the other hand, he also continues to search for a way to return Nezuko to human form.

The main battle involves Kibutsuji Muzan, the leader of the demons and the mastermind behind many tragedies. With the help of the Pillars, elite demon slayers, Tanjiro and his comrades face increasingly powerful enemies. The conflict between good and evil, and Tanjiro's struggle to keep Nezuko alive, is at the heart of the story.

The story not only presents action and adventure, but also values ​​about family, sacrifice, and hope. The stunning animation and deep character development make Demon Slayer one of the most beloved anime series.
