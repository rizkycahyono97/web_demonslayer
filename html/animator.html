A Bit About Me

I Am Japanese manga artist, known for the manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

I am Koyoharu Gotōge, a Japanese manga artist best known as the creator of the highly successful series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. While my real name and birthdate are not publicly known, I prefer to maintain a certain level of anonymity. My journey as a manga artist began with my debut one-shot Kagarigari in 2013, which introduced readers to some of the darker themes that would later be explored in Demon Slayer.
