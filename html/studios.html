Anime Film

Anime films are feature-length animated productions that are released in theaters. They often have higher animation quality than TV series or OVAs, due to their larger budgets and longer production times. Ufotable is known for creating anime films that serve not only as visual entertainment, but also as immersive cinematic experiences.

Includes:

  • Sakura no Ondo
  • Majocco Shimai no Yoyo to Nene
  • Tales of Zestiria: Dawn of the Shepherd
  • Witch on the Holy Night
  • The Garden of Sinners: Future Gospel
  • Fate/stay night: Heaven's Feel I. presage flower

Vaporware

Vaporware from Ufotable refers to animated projects or works that were announced but never released, delayed indefinitely, or even canceled without further explanation. As a renowned studio, Ufotable has had several projects that were eagerly awaited but never realized for various reasons, such as production constraints, priorities on other projects, or strategic decisions.

Includes:

  • Girls' Work
  • Katsugeki/Touken Ranbu film
